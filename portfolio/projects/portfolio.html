As a part of the Prasix II course, the students were required to create an Engineering Design Portfolio to demonstrate their areas of strength, progress, and future development as an engineering designer. As a dedicated coder, I've decided that I'd make mine in the form of a website from the group up with good'ol HTML5 and JQuery (JavaScript).

Key Features:

  • Demonstrates creative design skills.
  • Shows personal design philosophies and projects.

Design Challenges:

As a designer, I take pride in my creative ability to give life to elegant and intuitive designs. This personal website is another perfect training group for my design abilities. My core philosoplies for this design project are the following:

  • It should be visually simple
  • It needs to be interactively intuitive
  • It should be visually pleasing
  • It needs to be consistent (<-- But this really goes for any asthetic design)
